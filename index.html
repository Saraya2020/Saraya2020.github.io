My Collaborative Bright Idea

A Salon that has a Daycare, Cafe, Hair Store and a Nail Salon. All in one

About



My idea was to have a hair and nail shop and a spa to have the day to themselves and feel good about themselves. Also for the single mothers it would be a day care for the kids so you know the mothers could have some time to themselves, and do their own thing and feel like they have the day to themselves for a minute. A little cafe will also be included, I know sometimes when I go get my hair done it is early in the morning and my mom doesn't have time to stop to get me breakfast so it'll just be at the shop when you get there.



Our goals

Goal #1

My goal is to make mothers feel special and just people and general because I know that taking care of children is not easy.

Goal #2

To allow single mothers to breathe for a minute without their kids beside them and actually feel like they are relaxed.

Goal #3

Provide the needs and care of single mothers such as daycare for the kids to play have fun, If you get hungry we have a cafe, a spa to feel relaxed.




My Purpose



To meet the needs and requirements of every customer and of the people that work for me (employees). It serves single mothers, women, men and children. Does the service for single mothers who need help with their kids and to break.
